# StyleSwap – AI-powered sustainable fashion assistant

Final project for the Building AI course


## Summary

StyleSwap is an AI-powered app that helps users reduce fast fashion consumption by recommending outfits from their existing wardrobe and enabling clothing swaps. The goal is to promote sustainable fashion while saving money and reducing waste.


## Background

Fast fashion has a significant negative impact on the environment and encourages overconsumption. Many people feel like they have nothing to wear, even though their closets are full, leading to unnecessary purchases.

This project aims to address:
* Overconsumption of clothing
* Textile waste and environmental damage
* Lack of awareness of existing wardrobe options

This problem is very common, especially among young adults and students. Personally, I am interested in both fashion and sustainability and wanted to combine these topics into a practical solution. The topic is important because fashion is one of the most polluting industries globally.


## How is it used?

Users upload images of their clothes into the app. The AI analyzes items (e.g. type, color, style) and suggests outfit combinations. Users can also swap clothes with others who have similar styles.

Typical use cases:
* Choosing an outfit for the day
* Rediscovering unused clothing
* Finding people to exchange clothes with

Target users:
* Students and young adults
* Sustainability-conscious consumers
* People interested in fashion but wanting to reduce spending



## Data sources and AI methods

### Data sources
* User-uploaded wardrobe images
* Clothing metadata (color, category, season)
* User preferences (style, occasions)
* Optional: second-hand platforms (e.g. resale apps)

### AI methods
* Computer Vision → recognizing clothing items from images
* Recommendation systems → suggesting outfits
* Clustering / similarity models → matching users with similar styles
